By the end of [the first week](http://fireant.github.io/unity3d_course.html), we get to install and run a sample project in Unity. The Linux version of Unity doesn't come with the sample project. To follow the lesson, you need to create an empty project. Then from the assets store, can download the sample assets which can be loaded in our empty project. That's basically the same as the sample project that comes with the Windows and OSX versions. The lessons have a rather slow pace, I watched them at x2 speed. The great thing is that the instructor touches many topics. After starting the lessons of the second week, I felt that the lessons and the whole course are well planed. That's probably due to the instructor's long experience in teaching. In another post I will write my review of the second week lessons.
